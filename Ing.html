-GEOGRAPHY:

•Developed Countries(países desenvolvidos)=United States, Canada, South Korea…
The caracteristics of a developed countrie are high life expectation and a low birth rate. They normaly have a lot of industries, the most part of the population stay on the urban areas.
•Developing Countries(países subdesenvolvidos): Angola, Brazil, Bolivia…
They normaly have a big social inequality, low life expectation and big birth rate. Have more urban areas.
•Contrast between Latina América and Africa: Developed cities in Latin America generally have more diversified economies and better infrastructure than many developed cities in Africa. Access to basic services, urbanization, culture, and language vary between the two regions.
•Contrast between developed and developing countries: The contrast is the birth rate and life expectation, lack of planning on the developing citys.
